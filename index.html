ℹ️ About Adless OS

🛡️
What is Adless OS?

Adless OS is a free, community-driven entertainment hub built for students and casual users who want access to games, movies, and shows without intrusive advertisements. We believe entertainment should be accessible to everyone, regardless of their ability to pay.

🌍 Where are we from?

Adless OS is an independent project developed in Argentina 🇦🇷. This is a personal, non-commercial project created by a hobbyist developer with no corporate backing, no venture funding, and no profit motive. All content is aggregated from publicly available sources on the internet for educational and entertainment purposes.

🎮 What do we offer?
  • 250+ browser-based games from publicly available sources
  • Movies and TV shows for educational and personal viewing
  • A live community chat and social features
  • Leaderboards and a badge achievement system
  • Zero mandatory ads for registered members
  • Fully free — no subscriptions, no paywalls

🔒 Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026 · Operated from Argentina 🇦🇷

1. Information We Collect

When you create an account, we collect your chosen username, a hashed (encrypted) password, and an optional email address used only for account recovery. We do not collect your real name, address, phone number, or payment information. We never sell your data to third parties.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used solely to operate the platform — to authenticate your account, display your username in chat and on leaderboards, and save your game preferences and progress. Your email (if provided) is only used for password recovery and is never shared or displayed publicly.

3. Cookies & Local Storage

We use browser localStorage to save your session, settings (theme, background, device mode), favorites, and recent games. No tracking cookies are set by Adless OS itself. Third-party embedded games may set their own cookies — we have no control over those.

4. Third-Party Services

We use Supabase (a cloud database provider) to store account data, chat messages, scores, and badges. Supabase is GDPR-compliant. We also embed games from third-party platforms (azgames.io, 1games.io, etc.) and videos from Google Drive. These third parties have their own privacy policies. We use Google Analytics (gtag.js) for anonymous traffic analytics — no personally identifiable information is shared.

5. Data Retention & Deletion

You can delete your account at any time from Settings → Delete Account. This permanently removes your profile, scores, messages, and badges from our database. If you wish to request manual deletion, email us at [email protected].

6. Children's Privacy

Adless OS is intended for general audiences. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

7. Security

Passwords are never stored in plain text — they are hashed using SHA-256 before being stored. All data is transmitted over HTTPS. However, no system is 100% secure and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

8. Contact

For privacy-related questions or requests, contact us at:
[email protected]

⚖️ DMCA Policy

Last updated: March 2026 · Operated from Argentina 🇦🇷

Our Position

Adless OS is a non-commercial, independent project based in Argentina. We respect the intellectual property rights of others and expect our users to do the same. Adless OS does not host, upload, or store any copyrighted content on our servers. All games are embedded from third-party platforms, and all video content consists of links to files shared publicly on Google Drive by third parties.

How to Submit a DMCA Takedown Notice

If you are a copyright owner and believe that content accessible through Adless OS infringes your copyright, please send a written notice to our designated agent with the following information:

  • Your full legal name and contact information (email, address)
  • A description of the copyrighted work you claim has been infringed
  • The specific URL or description of the content on our site
  • A statement that you have a good faith belief that the use is not authorized
  • A statement, under penalty of perjury, that the information is accurate and you are the copyright owner or authorized to act on their behalf
  • Your electronic or physical signature

Counter-Notice

If you believe your content was removed by mistake or misidentification, you may submit a counter-notice to the same email address. Counter-notices must include your contact information, identification of the removed content, a statement under penalty of perjury that you have a good faith belief the content was removed by mistake, and your consent to jurisdiction.

⚠️ Limitation of Liability

Adless OS operates as a link aggregator and embedding platform. We do not upload, transcode, or redistribute copyrighted works. We act in good faith to remove content upon receiving valid notices. As a non-commercial project operated from Argentina, we operate under significant resource constraints but are committed to honoring legitimate intellectual property claims.
